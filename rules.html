Discord server

  1. Be respectful to everyone. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  2. No spamming in any of the channels. Keep the conversation relevant and on-topic.
  3. Avoid sharing personal information or doxxing others.
  4. Do not post NSFW content or anything that may be inappropriate for younger audiences.
  5. Keep discussions in the appropriate channels. Use the correct channels for specific topics.
  6. No self-promotion or advertising without permission from the server staff.
  7. Listen to and respect the decisions of the server moderators and admins.
  8. If you encounter any issues, please report them to the staff instead of trying to handle them yourself.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
